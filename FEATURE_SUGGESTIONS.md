# Feature Suggestions & Competitor Analysis

## üéØ Additional Features That Would Be Great

### 1. **AI-Powered Features**
- **AI Explanation**: Deep explanations of concepts, grammar, cultural context
- **Understanding Check**: User explains in own words, AI provides feedback
- **Question Generation**: AI creates comprehension questions from reading
- **Summarization**: AI summarizes chapters/sections
- **Contextual Help**: AI explains why certain words are used in specific contexts

### 2. **Social & Sharing**
- **Shared Libraries**: Share book recommendations with friends
- **Study Groups**: Form groups to read and discuss together
- **Leaderboards**: Compete with friends on vocabulary learned
- **Achievements**: Unlock badges for milestones (100 words, 10 books, etc.)

### 3. **Advanced Reading Tools**
- **Reading Speed Tracker**: Track WPM and improve over time
- **Reading Goals**: Set daily/weekly reading targets
- **Reading Streaks**: Gamify daily reading habits
- **Book Progress**: Visual progress bars for each book
- **Reading Statistics**: Time spent reading, words learned, books completed

### 4. **Enhanced Vocabulary Management**
- **Custom Lists**: Create themed word lists (business, travel, etc.)
- **Word Relationships**: Show synonyms, antonyms, related words
- **Word Frequency**: Highlight most common words to learn first
- **Pronunciation Practice**: Audio playback and recording
- **Example Sentences**: Multiple example sentences per word

### 5. **Export & Import**
- **Export to Anki**: Export vocabulary as Anki decks
- **Export to CSV/JSON**: For backup and analysis
- **Import from Other Apps**: Import from Quizlet, Memrise, etc.
- **Print Flashcards**: Physical flashcards for offline study

### 6. **Advanced Review Features**
- **Adaptive Difficulty**: Automatically adjust based on performance
- **Review Reminders**: Smart notifications based on your schedule
- **Review Analytics**: See which words you struggle with most
- **Batch Review**: Review multiple words at once
- **Voice Review**: Speak answers instead of typing

## üåê Competitors & Similar Apps

### **Vocabulary & Spaced Repetition**
1. **Anki** - Most popular spaced repetition app
   - Pros: Highly customizable, powerful algorithm
   - Cons: Steep learning curve, not reading-focused

2. **Memrise** - Gamified vocabulary learning
   - Pros: Great UI, video content, community features
   - Cons: Less reading-focused, subscription model

3. **Quizlet** - Flashcard and study tools
   - Pros: Huge community, easy to use
   - Cons: Not reading-integrated, basic algorithm

4. **LingQ** - Reading-based language learning
   - Pros: Extensive library, reading-focused
   - Cons: Expensive, less gamified

5. **Readlang** - Web reader with vocabulary
   - Pros: Simple, web-based, good for reading
   - Cons: Limited features, basic UI

### **Reading Companions**
6. **Kindle Vocabulary Builder** - Built into Kindle
   - Pros: Integrated with reading
   - Cons: Limited features, Amazon ecosystem only

7. **Pleco** - Chinese learning (but concept applies)
   - Pros: Excellent dictionary integration
   - Cons: Language-specific

8. **Beelinguapp** - Bilingual reading
   - Pros: Side-by-side translation, audio
   - Cons: Limited language pairs

### **Comprehensive Language Learning**
9. **Duolingo** - Gamified language learning
   - Pros: Highly gamified, addictive, free
   - Cons: Not reading-focused, repetitive

10. **Babbel** - Structured courses
    - Pros: Well-structured, professional
    - Cons: Not reading-focused, expensive
